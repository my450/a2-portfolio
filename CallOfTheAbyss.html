In this project, we designed the UI for a landing page for a concert called "Call of the Abyss". This is a fictional underwater fundraising music concert aimed to provide users with information and the opportunity to participate in this concert and contribute to a friendly ecosystem by buying merchandise and tickets. Therefore, the concert can be held successfully and the profits can be used to conserve the ocean by planting corals. In addition, the visual identity we created for the website also helped serve our event’s purpose.
